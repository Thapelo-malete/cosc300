Frequently Asked Questions

What is the purpose of this app?

The app is designed to help users identify signs of suicidal ideation in their social media posts or messages and provide resources for suicide prevention. It also provides real-time notifications of crime hotspot areas to enhance safety.

How can I report crime or suspicious activity through the app?

The app integrates with mapping services to identify crime hotspot areas in real-time. If you are in one of these areas, the app will notify you and provide a way to report any suspicious activity.

Are there professionals available to help me if I am feeling suicidal?

Yes, the app provides contact details for individuals with expertise in helping suicidal people.

Is my information safe and secure on this app?

Yes, the app takes measures to ensure the privacy and security of user information. All data is encrypted and stored securely to prevent unauthorized access.

Can I use the app without creating an account?

Yes, the app can be used as a guest user without creating an account. However, creating an account will allow you to access additional features and support resources.
